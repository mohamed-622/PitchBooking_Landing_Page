About this project

  • Project Inspiration

    PitchBooking is a passion-driven initiative that originated from a simple yet impactful idea. In the pre-PitchBooking era, the process of booking a local stadium in my city was nothing short of a logistical challenge. Picture this: the need to physically be present at the venue or resort to manual inquiries via WhatsApp just to ascertain whether a desired time slot was available. The inefficiency and time-consuming nature of this method added an unnecessary layer of complexity to the already demanding task of scheduling sports activities. The frustration of navigating the complex process of reserving the stadium sparked the vision for a streamlined solution.

    As the concept matured, the desire to simplify and enhance the experience for local sports enthusiasts became the driving force. The journey from concept to code reflects the dedication to solving a real-world problem and making a positive impact on the community.

  • Inception to Implementation

    PitchBooking's roots extend beyond the Alx Portfolio Project, originating from a desire to streamline local stadium reservations. The official journey began in week one of the Alx timeline, marked by continuous learning, iterative development, and overcoming challenges.

    The timeline vividly illustrates the evolution of PitchBooking from a concept to a fully functional web application. It's more than just a project; it's a journey of turning an idea into a practical solution, making stadium reservations easier and more efficient. And it is also my first project!!

  • Holberton School Portfolio Project

    PitchBooking is a proud addition to my portfolio, representing the culmination of skills acquired at Holberton School. This project not only demonstrates technical proficiency but also serves as a testament to the learning experience gained during my time at Holberton.
